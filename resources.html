Background Materials

This One Page Introduction is intended to introduce the Forum to business sponsors.

Our Charter does two things: First, it outlines the vision, objectives, conventions, membership, and commitments of “The ISO 20022 Forum for Architects” (hereafter “The Forum”), and second, it sets clear expectations for members and other interested parties regarding the activities and outputs of The Forum.

Important Note: By participating in the Forum and by accessing Forum resources, participants agree to the principles and conventions outlined in our charter.

So, you want to join the Forum?

The Forum welcomes members who are:

  • Architect profiles working for financial institutions, and
  • Familiar with the ISO 20022 standard, and
  • Ready to make active contributions to the work of The Forum, and
  • Willing to abide by the principles and conventions documented in the attached charter.

Forum membership, including full access to all Forum resources is otherwise entirely free of charge and obligation.

So, if you’re a [Systems | Enterprise | Information | Data | IT | Integration | Software | Solutions | Business | …] Architect who meets the above criteria, request membership now by sending a mail to the Secretariat to introduce yourself.
